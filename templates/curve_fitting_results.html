💡 Understanding Your Results
›
IC50: The dilution at which 50% neutralisation occurs (in log2 scale). More negative values indicate higher neutralising activity.
›
R²: Goodness of fit measure (0-1). Values above 0.5 indicate reasonable fits; below 0.5 are treated as flat curves.
›
Quality: "Good" fits have parameters within normal ranges. "Unstable" fits have parameters at boundary limits and should be interpreted with caution.
›
Flat curves: Samples with R² < 0.5 or failed fits are displayed as horizontal lines at their mean neutralisation value.
